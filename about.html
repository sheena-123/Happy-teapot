Our Story

Founded in 2020, Happy Teapot started as a small dream to bring the warmth of traditional tea ceremonies to modern life. We source the finest leaves and beans from around the world, ensuring every cup is a journey of flavor and comfort. Whether you're seeking a quiet afternoon tea or a bold coffee kick, our passionate team is here to serve you with a smile.

Our Mission

To create a welcoming space where every customer feels at home, savoring beverages that nourish the soul and foster connections. We are committed to sustainability, quality, and community.
